Who We Are

Inter Niagara FC is a community-driven soccer club established with the goal of promoting personal and community development through sport. Our club provides a welcoming environment where individuals can enhance their physical, mental, and social well-being while learning valuable leadership skills.

Our Mission

  • To build and develop character, foster responsible citizenship, and encourage sportsmanship among our members by offering opportunities for growth through the game of soccer.
  • Assist newly immigrants in seamlessly integrating into Canadian society by providing expert career advice, comprehensive training, and personalized coaching

Our Values

  • Unity: We believe in the power of teamwork and collaboration.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and fairness on and off the field.
  • Community: We are dedicated to giving back and supporting our local community.

Our Objectives

  • Promote sportsmanship and healthy competition.
  • Provide opportunities for physical, mental, and social development.
  • Operate as a not-for-profit organization that reinvests in the community.
